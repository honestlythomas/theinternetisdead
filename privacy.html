Privacy

A plain-English privacy note for theinternetisdead.org.

+--------------------------------------------------+ | THE INTERNET IS DEAD. YOUR BROWSER REMEMBERS. | +--------------------------------------------------+

The short version:

This site does not use accounts, logins, or server-side user profiles.

Most saved weirdness lives in your own browser through localStorage or sessionStorage. Clear your browser data and the ghost forgets you.

What This Site Stores

This site may store small pieces of information in your browser so the site can remember what you were doing. Revolutionary stuff, apparently.

That may include things like:

  • settings and interface preferences
  • theme or visual customization choices
  • game state, stats, or progress
  • nicknames or profile-like labels you choose locally
  • chat/interface preferences for local command systems
  • saved experimental page states
  • other tiny haunted crumbs needed for the site to function

Browser Storage

This site mostly uses browser-side storage, such as localStorage and sessionStorage.

localStorage can stick around after you close the browser.

sessionStorage usually disappears when the tab or session ends.

This data stays in your browser unless you export it, share it, upload it somewhere, or your browser/device does something exciting and terrible with it.

What This Site Does Not Intentionally Collect

This site does not intentionally collect account data, passwords, real names, addresses, payment information, or personal profiles on a private server.

This site is a static web art swamp, not a social network, bank, hospital, or surveillance goblin in a blazer.

Cookies

This site does not intentionally rely on its own cookies for user accounts or tracking.

Third-party embeds or links may behave differently, because the modern web is a stack of smaller websites hiding inside larger websites like cursed nesting dolls.

Third-Party Stuff

Some pages may link to or embed third-party content, including video-related content such as YouTube.

Those third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Once you interact with an embedded video, external link, or third-party frame, you may be dealing with that service too.

This site cannot control what YouTube, GitHub, your browser, your extensions, your device, your internet provider, or other external services do. Humanity built this arrangement and then called it convenience.

Clearing Your Data

You can clear this site's saved local data by clearing browser storage/site data for theinternetisdead.org in your browser settings.

Doing that may delete saved settings, game progress, local profiles, visual customizations, and other browser-stored data.

In other words: clear the data and the ghost forgets you. Sometimes that is mercy.

Sensitive Information

Do not enter sensitive personal information into experimental tools on this site.

This site is an art project and personal web experiment. Treat it like a haunted arcade cabinet, not a secure vault.

Changes

This privacy note may change as the site changes. If new features are added, especially anything involving storage, embeds, uploads, or external services, this page should be updated too. Astonishingly, documentation has to follow reality.
